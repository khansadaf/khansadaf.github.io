We use your data only for the following purposes:

Section 04

Recordings — Foreground Only, Zero-Knowledge

Safe never records audio in the background. Recording only happens when the app is open and visible on your screen and you explicitly tap the Record button. There is always a visible recording indicator on screen. We cannot access your recordings — this is not a policy, it is a technical fact.

All recordings are encrypted on your device before any cloud upload. We store only the encrypted file and do not hold the decryption key. Even if legally compelled, we cannot provide recording content.

Microphone Permission

The microphone permission is used only when you explicitly start a recording with the app open on your screen. Safe does not use the microphone in the background. If you deny microphone permission, all other features continue to work normally.

Your Responsibility

Recording laws vary by jurisdiction. You are solely responsible for ensuring your use of recording features complies with local law. In many countries you may record conversations you are a party to for your personal safety. We encourage you to check your local laws before recording.

Recording Limits

  • Free users: Up to 10 recordings stored locally on device only. No cloud upload.
  • Pro users: Unlimited recordings, backed up to encrypted cloud storage (5 GB). Shareable by you only.
Section 05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

With your trusted contacts (your choice)

When you trigger an SOS, your location is shared with your chosen trusted contacts via SMS. This is the core function of the App and you control who your trusted contacts are.

With service providers

We use a small number of carefully selected service providers who are bound by strict data processing agreements:

  • Google Firebase — account authentication, database, cloud storage, push notifications. Governed by Google's data processing terms.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Receives your user ID and subscription status only.
  • Google Maps Platform — map display and nearby places lookup. No personal data shared beyond necessary API calls.

Legal requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority. Given our zero-knowledge architecture, we can provide: account existence confirmation, phone number used for registration, timestamps of SOS events (metadata only — no content). We cannot provide recordings or encrypted data regardless of the request.

We will notify you of any such request where legally permitted to do so.

Business transfers

If Safe is acquired or merged with another entity, your data would be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified and given the option to delete your account before any transfer occurs.

Section 10

Security Measures

We take security seriously. Measures in place include:

  • Zero-knowledge encryption for all recordings — encrypted on device, we hold no keys
  • Firebase Security Rules — strict rules ensure users can only read and write their own data; no developer admin access to individual user content
  • HTTPS / TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Biometric / PIN protection — app requires authentication to access sensitive screens
  • Trusted contact verification — contacts must opt in via confirmation SMS before receiving any alerts
  • No plain-text storage of sensitive data on our servers

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours (as required by GDPR and applicable laws) via the contact information associated with your account.
