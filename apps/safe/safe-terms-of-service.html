Section 03

What Safe Does

Safe is a personal safety application that provides tools to help users in potentially dangerous situations. Features include:

  • SOS Alerts: Send your location via SMS to pre-selected trusted contacts
  • Guardian Mode: Background GPS location monitoring and shake-trigger detection
  • Manual Recording: Record audio when the app is open and visible, with an explicit tap — foreground only, never background
  • Fake Call: Simulate an incoming phone call to exit uncomfortable situations
  • Journey Timer: Set arrival timers with automated alerts to contacts if you don't check in
  • Emergency Dial: Quick access to local emergency numbers, auto-detected by country
  • Location Sharing: Share real-time location with trusted contacts
  • Nearby Help: Show nearest police stations, hospitals, and shelters on a map
Important: Safe is a supplementary safety tool. It is NOT a substitute for local emergency services. In any life-threatening emergency, always call your local emergency number (112 in India, 911 in the US, 999 in the UK) first. Safe does not guarantee response times, delivery of alerts, or the availability of any specific feature at any time.

Our Access to Recordings

We do not access, listen to, view, or process your recordings. All recordings are encrypted on your device before any cloud upload (Pro users). We operate a zero-knowledge architecture — we do not hold encryption keys and cannot access your recordings under any circumstances, including in response to third-party requests.

How Recording Works — Foreground Only

Safe only records audio when the app is open and visible on your screen and you have explicitly tapped the record button. Safe does never record audio in the background without your active, visible interaction. The app will always display a clear recording indicator on screen whenever recording is active.

Recording Storage Limits

  • Free users: Up to 10 recordings stored locally on your device. Oldest recordings are automatically deleted when the limit is reached. No cloud upload.
  • Pro users: Up to 5 GB cloud backup plus unlimited local storage. Cloud recordings are end-to-end encrypted.
