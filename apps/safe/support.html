Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Safe need my location?

Location is used to power SOS alerts and live-location sharing with the trusted contacts and family members you choose. It is never shared publicly.

Why does Safe ask for camera access?

Only to scan a family member's QR "Safe Code" so you can link accounts. No photos or video are captured or stored.

Why does Safe ask for contacts access?

Only so you can quickly pick a name and number when adding a trusted contact. Your phonebook is never uploaded.

How do I delete my account and data?

Open the app and go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated data.

How do I cancel my Safe+ subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings, not inside the app directly.
