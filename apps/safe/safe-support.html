Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the SOS button work?
When you press the SOS button, Safe starts a 3-second countdown (which you can cancel). After the countdown, it sends an SMS message containing your name, a distress message, and a Google Maps link to your current GPS location to all your trusted contacts simultaneously. An internet connection is not required for SMS alerts — they work on mobile network only.
What is Guardian Mode?
Guardian Mode is Safe's background protection feature. When active, it continuously monitors your GPS location. Your location is updated every 30 seconds and shared with trusted contacts if you activate sharing. Free users get 30 minutes of Guardian Mode per day. Pro users get unlimited time. You must explicitly start and stop Guardian Mode — it does not activate automatically. A persistent notification is always shown while Guardian Mode is running.
Does Safe work without internet?
Core safety features work without internet: SOS via SMS, emergency dial, fake call, and locally stored safety tips. Features that require internet: live location share link and nearby police/hospital lookup. We strongly recommend enabling SMS permissions to ensure SOS works in all conditions.
Can I use Safe on multiple devices?
Yes. Your Safe account is tied to your Google account. Sign in with the same Google account on any device and your Pro subscription and contacts will be restored automatically within a few seconds. Note that audio recordings are device-local only and do not sync — they stay only on the device that recorded them.
I accidentally triggered SOS — what do I do?
Safe gives you a 3-second countdown before sending any SOS alert. Tap anywhere on the screen during the countdown to cancel. If an alert was already sent, contact your trusted contacts immediately to let them know it was accidental. There is no way for us to recall an SMS that has already been delivered.
How do I add or change trusted contacts?
Go to the Contacts tab in the app. Tap "Add Contact" and select from your phone book or enter a number manually. The contact will receive a confirmation SMS and must reply to confirm they consent to receiving SOS alerts from you. Free users can add up to 5 contacts. Pro users can add up to 10. Contacts can be removed at any time from the same screen (biometric verification required).
Can people listen to my recordings?
No. Safe only records when you explicitly tap the Record button with the app open on your screen — never in the background. A recording indicator is always visible while recording. Recordings stay only on your device and are never uploaded anywhere, and are only accessible by you after biometric or PIN verification. The Safe developer has zero access to your recordings — this is a technical limitation, not just a policy, since there is no copy on any server. Sharing recordings is available to Pro users only, and only you can initiate a share.
How do I cancel my Safe Pro subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple App Store or Google Play, not directly by Safe. To cancel: on iPhone, go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Safe → Cancel. On Android, go to Google Play → Menu → Subscriptions → Safe → Cancel. You will retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period. Deleting your Safe account does NOT cancel billing — you must cancel through your device settings.
What happens when I delete my account?
All data is permanently and immediately deleted: your Firebase account, all Firestore data, your RevenueCat subscription record, and all local app data on your device (including audio recordings, which are never stored anywhere but your device). This cannot be undone. Remember to cancel your App Store or Google Play subscription separately before deleting your account, otherwise billing will continue.
Why does Safe need microphone permission?
The microphone is used only when you explicitly tap the Record button with the Safe app open on your screen. Safe never listens to or records audio in the background. If you tap Record, a clear recording indicator appears on screen at all times. You can use every other feature in Safe — SOS, Guardian Mode, emergency dial, fake call, journey timer — without granting microphone permission at all.
Why does Safe need location permission?
Location is used to include your GPS coordinates in SOS alerts, for live location sharing with trusted contacts, and to show nearby police stations and hospitals on the map. Location is never shared without your explicit action or active Guardian Mode. We recommend granting "Always Allow" for Guardian Mode to work correctly in background — this is clearly explained during onboarding.
Is Safe available in my country?
Safe is available globally on both the Apple App Store and Google Play. Emergency numbers are automatically detected based on your device's locale and region settings. Core features work worldwide. Some features such as the nearby help finder rely on Google Maps data which may have varying accuracy in different regions.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Fixes

SOS alert not sending

1
Check SMS permissions. Go to your phone Settings → Apps → Safe → Permissions → SMS → Allow.
2
Check trusted contacts are verified. Contacts must have confirmed via SMS before they receive alerts. Check the Contacts screen for "Pending" status.
3
Check mobile signal. SMS requires at least 1 bar of signal. SOS cannot send via SMS with zero signal.
4
Check Do Not Disturb. DND mode on some devices can block outgoing SMS. Disable DND when using Safe.

Guardian Mode stops in background

1
Android — disable battery optimisation. Settings → Battery → Battery Optimisation → Safe → Don't Optimise. This is the most common cause.
2
Android — allow background activity. Settings → Apps → Safe → Battery → Unrestricted.
3
iOS — enable Background App Refresh. Settings → General → Background App Refresh → Safe → On.
4
Keep screen on or use the Guardian Mode notification. Tapping the persistent notification keeps the service alive on Android.

Biometric / PIN not working

1
Use your device's registered biometric. Safe uses your phone's built-in Face ID or fingerprint — the same one used to unlock your phone.
2
Use your Safe PIN as backup. If biometric fails 3 times, Safe will offer your PIN. If you forgot your PIN, contact support.
3
Re-enrol biometric. If you recently updated your fingerprint or Face ID on your device, go to Settings → App Lock → Re-enable and set up again.
Account

Account & Data Management

How to delete your account

Go to Settings → Account Actions → Delete Account. You will be asked to re-authenticate, optionally provide a reason, and confirm by typing "DELETE." All your data is permanently erased immediately. Remember to cancel your subscription separately in App Store or Google Play settings.

How to export your data

To request a copy of your personal data, email meehumsak.support@gmail.com from the Google account email associated with your Safe account. We will prepare and send your data within 30 days.

Forgot your Safe PIN

If you have forgotten your Safe PIN, contact support at meehumsak.support@gmail.com from the Google account email associated with your Safe account. We will verify your identity via that email and assist you in resetting your PIN. We cannot bypass biometric verification on your behalf.

Subscription and billing issues

For refund requests or billing disputes, contact Apple Support (for iPhone users) or Google Play Support (for Android users) directly — billing is managed entirely by them. For questions about your Pro plan status within the app, contact meehumsak.support@gmail.com.

Legal

Consent, Safety, and Your Rights

Recording consent

When you first activate Guardian Mode, Safe displays a consent screen explaining that audio recording will occur. You must acknowledge this screen to proceed. Recording only happens when you have explicitly started Guardian Mode. You are responsible for complying with recording consent laws in your country.

Reporting misuse or abuse

If you believe someone is misusing Safe to harass you — for example, by adding you as a trusted contact without your knowledge — please contact us at meehumsak.support@gmail.com. Include the phone number that added you and a description of the issue. We take abuse reports very seriously and will act promptly.

Law enforcement requests

Because recordings are never stored anywhere but your own device, we have no recording content to provide under any circumstance. We can confirm account existence and provide metadata (timestamps, not content) only in response to a valid court order. For legal correspondence: meehumsak.support@gmail.com.

Your rights under GDPR, India DPDP Act, and CCPA: You have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data. Contact meehumsak.support@gmail.com for any data rights request. We respond within 30 days.
