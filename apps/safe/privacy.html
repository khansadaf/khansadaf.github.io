Safe Privacy Policy

Effective date: 19 July 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Safe app ("Application"), a personal safety app for mobile devices, created by Sadaf Khan ("Service Provider"). This service is provided "AS IS" and is intended to help you share your location and status with people you trust during an emergency.

Information We Collect

Safe is a safety app, so most of what it collects is data you choose to share with people you add to your safety circle (trusted contacts and family members). We collect:

  • Account information — email address / phone number, via Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In, to create and secure your account.
  • Precise location — used for SOS alerts, live-location sharing, and Guardian Mode. Your location is shared only with the trusted contacts and family members you explicitly add — never made public or sold.
  • Camera — used only to scan a family member's QR "Safe Code" to link accounts. The camera is never used to capture or store photos/video, and only activates after you grant permission.
  • Contacts (phonebook) — read-only access, used only when you tap "Choose from Contacts" while adding a trusted contact, to pre-fill a name and number you select. Your phonebook is never uploaded or scanned in the background.
  • Microphone / audio — Guardian Mode and SOS alerts can record short audio clips as safety evidence. Recordings are encrypted and are visible only to you and the trusted contacts you've shared that alert with.
  • Device & usage data — device model, OS version, app version, and general usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics), used to keep the app secure, diagnose crashes, and improve features.
  • Push notification tokens — via Firebase Cloud Messaging, used to deliver SOS and family-alert notifications.
  • Subscription/purchase data — if you subscribe to Safe+, your purchase is processed directly by the Apple App Store or Google Play, and receipt validation is handled by RevenueCat. We never see or store your card details.

How Your Data Is Used

Data is used solely to power Safe's safety features: delivering SOS alerts, sharing live location with people you choose, powering Guardian Mode monitoring, managing your family/trusted-contacts circle, and keeping your account secure. We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

Sharing With Trusted Contacts & Family

Location, SOS alerts, and Guardian Mode data are shared only with the specific trusted contacts and family members you add inside the app — never with the public or unrelated third parties. You control who is in your safety circle and can remove someone at any time.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Data is stored using Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, and Cloud Storage), secured with access rules that restrict each user's data to their own account and the family/trusted-contact members they are mutually linked with. Audio recordings are encrypted before storage.

Third-Party Services

Safe relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Messaging, Analytics) — Google
  • Google Sign-In & Google Maps — Google
  • RevenueCat — subscription receipt management
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Billing — payment processing

Children & Family Accounts

Safe's primary account holder must be an adult. A parent or guardian may add a family member — including a minor — to their own safety circle from their own account; the parent/guardian is responsible for any consent required for that minor's inclusion. Safe does not knowingly allow a child to independently create their own primary account without a parent or guardian.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account inside the app, or by emailing us at the address below.

Security

We use physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards — including encryption and Firebase security rules — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data using industry-standard practices.

Changes to This Policy
